# TrustWall

TrustWall is a powerful social proof platform that helps businesses collect, organize, and display customer reviews across their entire sales funnel. It transforms authentic customer feedback into conversion-driving proof.

## The Problem

Businesses struggle to collect and showcase customer reviews in a way that genuinely builds trust and drives conversions. Scattered testimonials across different platforms make it hard to present a unified, compelling narrative of customer satisfaction.

## The Solution

TrustWall provides a centralized platform to collect both text and video reviews, organize them intelligently, and display them at the right touchpoints across your funnel — from landing pages to checkout flows.

## Key Features

- **Review Collection** — Easily collect text and video testimonials from customers through customizable forms
- **Smart Organization** — Tag, categorize, and filter reviews to display the right proof at the right time
- **Embeddable Widgets** — Drop review walls, carousels, and badges anywhere on your site
- **Video Testimonials** — Capture and display authentic video reviews for maximum impact
- **Analytics Dashboard** — Track how social proof impacts your conversion rates

## How It Works

1. Set up your review collection form and share it with customers
2. Reviews flow into your TrustWall dashboard for moderation
3. Organize reviews by product, tag, or rating
4. Embed beautiful review widgets on your site with a single line of code
5. Watch your conversion rates improve with authentic social proof
