# DrawMrr

DrawMrr lets indie hackers and SaaS founders turn their MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue) milestones into beautiful, share-ready visuals. Perfect for celebrating wins and sharing progress on social media.

## The Problem

Indie hackers love sharing their MRR milestones on Twitter/X, but creating visually appealing progress graphics is time-consuming. Most end up posting plain text or screenshots of dashboards that lack personality.

## The Solution

DrawMrr turns your MRR numbers into hand-drawn style progress bars and milestone graphics that stand out in social feeds. It's the fastest way to create beautiful MRR visuals.

## Key Features

- **Hand-Drawn Style** — Unique sketch-style progress bars that feel personal and authentic
- **Multiple Export Formats** — Export as PNG, JPG, animated GIF, or video
- **Customizable Themes** — Match your personal brand with different color schemes and styles
- **Milestone Markers** — Highlight key revenue milestones on your journey
- **Quick Share** — One-click sharing to Twitter/X and other platforms

## How It Works

1. Enter your MRR numbers and milestones
2. Choose a visual style and customize colors
3. Preview your hand-drawn MRR progress bar
4. Export as PNG, JPG, GIF, or video
5. Share your milestone with the world
