# DrawMRR

DrawMRR is a free tool that lets indie hackers and SaaS founders sketch their MRR journey as a hand-drawn progress bar — then export it as a PNG, JPG, animated GIF, or video to share anywhere.

## Why I Built This

Indie hackers love sharing MRR milestones on Twitter/X, but the options are either plain text numbers or boring dashboard screenshots. I wanted a quick way to turn revenue numbers into something that actually looks good in a feed — something with personality. So I built DrawMRR over a weekend.

## How It Works

1. Set your start, current, and goal MRR values.
2. Tweak the title, unit label, font, and color palette to match your style.
3. Preview the hand-drawn progress bar with a fill animation.
4. Hit export to grab a PNG, JPG, animated GIF, or video you can drop straight into a tweet, changelog, or investor update.

That's it. No sign-up, no paywall, no friction.

## Features

- **Hand-Drawn Style** — Progress bars rendered with a sketchy, rough aesthetic (powered by adjustable roughness) that feels personal and stands out from polished dashboards.
- **Customizable Everything** — Choose your font family, color palette, step count, unit labels, and title. Make it yours.
- **Fill Animation** — Run a satisfying fill animation to see your progress come to life. Great for screen recordings and GIFs.
- **Multiple Export Formats** — Export as PNG, JPG, animated GIF, or video. Whatever your platform needs.
- **Instant Preview** — See changes in real time as you adjust settings. No waiting, no rendering delays.

## The Build

DrawMRR was a one-to-two day build. It's a simple, focused tool — no backend, no auth, no database. Just a Next.js app with TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, and shadcn/ui for the controls. The canvas rendering handles the hand-drawn aesthetic, and the export pipeline converts it to multiple formats client-side.

I barely used AI in the build. It was a quick project where the scope was small enough to just sit down and ship it.

## Free Forever

DrawMRR is completely free. No accounts, no limits, no catches. Just open it, make your visual, and share your milestone.
